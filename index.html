The Borsch

A place to return to for the best Ukrainian borsch in Prague!

Our cozy restaurant was opened back 2021. As our main mission we saw the need to spread the word about the Ukrainian culture and one of its biggest achievements - soup called borsch. Some people say it's just some tasty soup, but we think there is more to it. The history, the symbol of true identity and the strongest nation.

If that sounds interesting to you, please consider stopping by for a bowl of our special elixir.
